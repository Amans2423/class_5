1️⃣ How does a Computer Store Information?

💬 A computer stores information in its memory, just like we store things in our school bag.

🎯 Simple Example:

  • When you save a photo, song, or document, the computer keeps it safe inside its memory.
  • It remembers where you saved it (like in a folder).

🔍 Little Technical (Easy to Understand):

  • When we create something on a computer (like typing a letter or making a drawing), it is first kept in the computer’s working memory called RAM.
  • But RAM is temporary. So, when we click "Save", the information moves to Secondary Memory like the Hard Disk.
  • Hard Disk stores the data permanently, even after the computer is switched off.

🧠 Real Life Example:

  • RAM is like your study table — books are kept temporarily while you study.
  • Hard Disk is like your school bag — you store your books there to carry them safely.
